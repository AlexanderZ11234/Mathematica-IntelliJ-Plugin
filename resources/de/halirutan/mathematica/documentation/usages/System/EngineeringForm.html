EngineeringForm

  • EngineeringForm[expr] prints with all real numbers in expr given in engineering notation.
  • EngineeringForm[expr,n] prints with numbers given to n ‐ digit precision.

Attributes: Protected

Options: DigitBlock, ExponentFunction, ExponentStep, NumberFormat, NumberMultiplier, NumberPadding, NumberPoint, NumberSeparator, NumberSigns, SignPadding
